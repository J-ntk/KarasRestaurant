Welcome

Karas Restaurant, nestled in the heart of Limenas, Thassos, is renowned for its authentic Greek cuisine and scenic views of the Aegean Sea. It offers a variety of fresh seafood and traditional dishes, all prepared with locally sourced ingredients. The friendly service and cozy atmosphere make it a favorite among both locals and tourists.

Karas Restaurant, nestled in the heart of Limenas, Thassos, is renowned for its authentic Greek cuisine and scenic views of the Aegean Sea. Established several years ago, Karas Restaurant has become a staple in Thassos for its dedication to preserving Greek culinary traditions. Over the years, it has built a reputation for high-quality food and a welcoming ambiance, attracting diners from around the world. The restaurant’s commitment to excellence has made it a beloved dining spot in Limenas.
